About Me, an Avid Non-Climber

I'm Jess, an armchair mountaineer. While I love hiking and the outdoors, there is never any chance of me climbing any sort of mountain where climbing skills are needed (as I have none), or where there is any risk of altitude sickness, avalanches, frostbite, or if there's a need to camp in temperatures below 10℃, or where I'd have to navigate heights. That definitely rules out most mountains above 3000 metres, and probably most that are less than that.

As is probably clear, I'm extremely risk averse, and that's no doubt why I'm slightly obsessed with the people who risk everything to get to the top of the world's highest peaks.
